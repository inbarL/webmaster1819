ABOUT US

Hi!! We’re the Weekend Wallflowers, an indie rock band that started in 2018 as a collaboration between three friends, three instruments, Sprite Cranberry™, and a love for music. Being only emotionally ridden high schoolers, we focus our themes around the transitions out of adolescence and explore, in effort to capitalize and exploit of our emotional turmoil, feelings of heartbreak and the dread that comes to living!! ( ^:

Emily

Emily is a vocalist and guitarist of the Wallflowers. Emily has been in showbusiness for years, but Em’s main passion is guitar; honing her pick as a weapon of choice since age 3. With her guitar and vocals, these elements dovetail beautifully with the other members’ talents and sweep audiences across the world.

Cammi

Cammi is the main vocalist and bassist and brings a clear and distinct voice to the band. “It’s always been a dream of mine to tour with my highschool garageband, but it never seemed realistic,” Cammi reflects, “Somehow everything has lined up and I’m enjoying every minute of it!”

Kit

Kit is the drummer of the Weekend Wallflowers. She along with the other wallflowers bring an excellent sound to the band with her deep-set tone and concepts. “I’ve played with a lot of people, but playing with my best friends,” She says, “has been the highlight of my musical career.”
