Teacher
James Martin teaches Introduction to Engineering Design and Engineering Design & Development at Boyd, helping students spark their interest in the engineering fields. He has been an educator for 14 years, with a Bachelor’s in Accounting, and Master’s in both Business & Administration, and Management & Information Sciences. Mr. Martin originally used to teach Microsoft Applications, and stepped into his current role as an engineering teacher since he was good with technology. It all worked out in the end since he is highly proficient with CAD programs, and students of all grades love diving into the projects in his classes.
